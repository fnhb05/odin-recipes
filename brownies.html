Fudgy Chewy Brownies

Description

These moist brownies are a crowd favorite. If you need a chocolate fix these babies will hit the spot. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup (64g) unsweetened cocoa, Dutch-process or natural
  • 8 tablespoons (113g) butter, melted and cooled
  • 1/4 cup (50g) vegetable oil
  • 2 cups (397g) sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder, optional; for enhanced chocolate flavor
  • 1 cup (120g) King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Lightly grease a 9" x 9" pan, or 8" x 8" pan (for slightly thicker brownies).
  2. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Spread the batter in the pan.
  4. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with some moist crumbs clinging to it, but doesn't show any trace of raw batter. Remove the pan from the oven and cool the brownies before cutting and serving.
