Macaroni and Cheese


Description

This hearty, homey dish can be a side or stand-alone. Feel free to be adventurous with different cheese combos to create your own flavor profile!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb box of maccaroni
  • 4c of shredded sharp cheddar
  • 3c shredded mild cheddar
  • 3c gruyere cheese
  • 2c colby cheese
  • parmasan to sprinkle on top
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 c milk
  • 1/2 c butter
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350ยบ. Bring large pot of water to boil and then salt generously. Cook macaroni just shy of package directions, al dente. Drain well and place in 9" by 13" baking dish or pan.
  2. In same pasta pot, whisk together whole milk, eggs, salt, pepper and until combined. Pour mixture over cooked macaroni in pan.
  3. Add butter, gruyere, Colby-Jack and sharp cheddar cheeses. Stir to combine well and top evenly with shredded mild cheddar.
  4. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned on top. Let rest 10 minutes or so before serving. Enjoy!
